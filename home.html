Smart Cyber Safety Guidance

Stay informed about common cyber threats and learn simple practices to protect your personal information, money, and online accounts from cyber criminals.

Key Safety Practices

  • ✓ Avoid sharing OTP or passwords with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank or service provider.
  • ✓ Be careful with unknown links received through email, messages, or social media.
  • ✓ Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts and enable two-factor authentication.
  • ✓ Verify payment requests carefully before approving any online transaction.
  • ✓ Keep your devices updated with the latest security updates and antivirus protection.
  • ✓ Report suspicious activities quickly to prevent further cyber attacks.

These tips help users stay safe online, protect their personal data, and reduce the risk of cyber crimes. 🛡️💻
